The V-taper is real

47cm shoulders, 104cm chest, 88cm waist — a 16cm drop. This is a strong athletic taper. Slim and tailored fits flatter it. Boxy or relaxed cuts hide it and add visual bulk you don't have.

Slim tapered Structured shoulder Boxy / oversized tops
👔
Shirts: the collar trap

Your 40cm neck takes a EU 40 collar. Many brands only go to 39 or 41 — always verify. In casual shirts this matters less, but a dress shirt collar that's too tight or gaping undermines everything else.

EU / cm 40 collar Charles Tyrwhitt Tall Check before buying online
🧥
Jackets: the 72cm body length

Your jacket length is 72cm — most EU 50 regular jackets run 74-76cm, which will be too long. EU 50 Short or a made-to-measure option may suit you better. Shoulder width (47cm) is your primary fit anchor; body length is secondary and can be altered.

Shoulder on point first EU 50 Short / check length Budget for body length alter
👖
Trousers: 83cm inseam

83cm (33") inseam is longer than most high-street stocks. With your 117cm out seam and 88cm waist, you need a tall-specific range or a brand offering L34 inseam. A 32" inseam works only with a deliberate turn-up or ankle stack.

ASOS Tall W34 L34 Gap Tall Reiss (measure inseam) Standard W34 L30/32
👞
UK 10 / EU 44 footwear

UK 10 is well-stocked by most brands. Your RM Williams dark brown Chelsea and Adidas Gazelle Indoor in camel/burgundy are both proportionally right for your height — a slim profile on a tall frame. Avoid overly chunky soles unless paired with slim trousers.

RM Williams (owned) Gazelle Indoor (owned) Chunky sole: use with care
🎨
Colour logic on your frame

Dark trouser, strong colour on top is your most powerful formula — it grounds the silhouette and lets your colouring lead. All-light reads washed out on a tall frame. A terracotta or teal shirt over espresso trousers is the visual sweet spot.

Strong colour up top Dark trouser base Head-to-toe light
